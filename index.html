one Minute Taoism

Posted on 2022-01-19

Taosim is a Chinese philosophy that was started by a dude that may not have been real. His name? Lao Zi. Thousands of years ago this mythical man had some crazy ideas that can now be found in the book, the Dao De Jing. He basically says that there is a naturally spontaneous and eternal order to the world that he calls the “Dao” or the “Way”. Guess what? We can tap in to this great “way” through another kind of way, “Wu wei.” Wu wei means action without action. confused yet? Well, to clear it up Lao Zi said: “Stop trying to leave and you will arrive. Stop seeking and you will see.” He also said: “If you try to change it, you will ruin it. Try to hold it and you will lose it.”

Why Do Owls Sleep Like That?

Posted on 2022-01-18

Owls are quite strange creatures. I’m fine, Slightly alien a little intimidating but funny. Yeah. They’re really funny. Just look at their long paws that look like your grandfather’s legs and underpants. Check out those feet. Man alive. Yeah. But that’s not the only thing about them. Little owls love to sleep on their stomachs. We have no idea why. But they can really sleep like this, like a very tired human cub where they fell, there’s a bed. And this is not an isolated incident. When the babies take a nap on a branch, they cling firmly to it with their claws so that they don’t accidentally fall and then they lie down on their stomachs.
